About me

From a young age I found a great passion in technology, being born and propelled into the era of modern technology, I was blessed to be able to get an iPad whilst my age was still in the single digits. This iPad was almost a one hit wonder for my life, and I'm not sure how it would be without it. It formed a lot of my early knowledge on how to program, and which later led to my engineering. Whilst lots of my GitHub work has been taken down, remnants of the past are visible.

Photography on the other hand, is the result of a lot of things. Lighting, colour, subject, but more importantly the vision, the vision that is to be captured. A moment in time, a glimpse of the past. I partake an interest in capturing that vision, and I want to help others remember things that should be unforgettable whether family, or the greatest day of your life. One day, the photos will be all you have left of someone or something.
